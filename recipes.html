Belgian Waffle

The Belgian waffle is a delightful treat, boasting its airy yet crispy texture that melts in your mouth. The batter is rich, light, and fluffy, creating a perfect base for any toppings.

This is the prep time clue!

prep time

20 min.

This is the cook time clue!

cook time

10 min.

This is the serving clue!

serving

4 servings

Instructions


step 1

Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.

step 2

In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

step 3

Separate the egg whites and yolks. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.

Ingredients


2 cups all-purpose flour; 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

1 tablespoon baking powder; 1/2 teaspoon salt

2 large eggs, separated; 1 3/4 cups milk

1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted; 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Shakshuka

Shakshuka is a flavorful dish made with poached eggs in a spicy tomato and pepper sauce, usually seasoned with various spices. It's a hearty and delicious meal that's perfect for breakfast or brunch.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

2 servings

Instructions


step 1

Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onions, peppers, and garlic. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

step 2

Stir in the spices and tomato paste. Cook for 2 minutes, then pour in the crushed tomatoes. Simmer for 10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.

step 3

Make small wells in the sauce and crack the eggs into them. Cover and cook until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny, about 5-7 minutes.

Ingredients


2 tablespoons olive oil

1 onion, diced; 1 red bell pepper, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced; 2 teaspoons paprika

1 teaspoon cumin; 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 tablespoon tomato paste; 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes; 4-6 large eggs

Salt and pepper to taste; Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Strawberry Banana Smoothie

The Strawberry Banana Smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious drink that combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with the tanginess of fresh strawberries, resulting in a delightful blend perfect for any time of day.

prep time

5 min.

cook time

0 min.

serving

2 servings

Instructions


step 1

Add the ripe bananas, fresh strawberries, and milk into a blender.

step 2

Blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy.

step 3

Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Strawberry Banana Smoothie!

Ingredients


2 ripe bananas

1 cup fresh strawberries

1 cup milk (any preferred type)

Optional: Honey or sweetener to taste

Ice cubes (optional)
